Lasagna

How to Make Lasagna

Making lasagna can be time consuming but well worth the effort. In this recipe you will find a full ingredients list and a step-by-step tutorial.

Ingredients

  • Meat: Italian sausage and lean ground beef.
  • Onion and garlic: An onion and two cloves of garlic.
  • Tomato products: Two cans of tomato sauce, two cans of tomato paste, and a can of crushed tomatoes.
  • Sugar: Two tablespoons of white sugar.
  • Spices and seasonings: Fresh parsley, dried basil leaves, salt, Italian seasoning, fennel seeds, and black pepper.
  • Lasagna noodles: Use store-bought or homemade lasagna noodles.
  • Cheeses: Parmesan, mozzarella, and ricotta cheese.
  • Egg: An egg helps to bind the ricotta.

How to Make Lasagna Step-By-Step

Here's a very brief overview of what you can expect when you make homemade lasagna:

  1. Make the meat sauce.
  2. Cook the noodles.
  3. Make the ricotta mixture.
  4. Layer the lasagna according to the recipe instructions below.
  5. Cover with foil and bake
  6. Let the lasagna rest before serving

How to Layer Lasagna

The order you will follow when layering your lasagna:

  1. Meat sauce
  2. Noodles
  3. Ricotta mixture
  4. Mozzarella slices
  5. Meat sauce
  6. Parmesan cheese
  7. Repeat the layers, then top with the remaining Parmesan
